About MindSpace

Anti-Social Media: You Are in Control

Here on MindSpace, we strive to provide a social media experience that allows you to view others as you perceive them. There is no need to view the accounts of other users; you can create profiles for them, allowing you complete control over your experience. Likewise, there is no need to create a profile for yourself; no one else would see it, silly! Our message function is currently a work in progress, but in our upcoming version, you will be able to control how your friends interact with you, too. Social media has never been so true to your experience and your experience alone.
