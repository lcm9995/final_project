About MindSpace

Welcome to MindSpace - AntiSocial Media for the Future

Imagine being able to use social media freely, without the inconvenience and stress of interacting with other real people...Here on MindSpace, we simulate the social media experience while letting you be in control. You can create the profiles of your friends, whether they really exist or not, and create them how YOU see them. Yup, you heard that right! Every MindSpace user's account and activity is completely invisible and inaccessable to other users!! Never worry about what others will think of you again, MindSpace is a safe space.
